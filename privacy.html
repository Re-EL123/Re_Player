Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 7, 2025

Re-Player ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable data unless explicitly provided during Google OAuth login. We collect usage preferences locally.

2. Use of Data

  • To access your Google Drive files (if authorized)
  • To personalize your playback and visual settings
  • To store favorites, themes, and EQ preferences locally

3. Data Sharing

We never sell, rent, or share your data. OAuth access is limited to the scope granted by you.

4. Local Storage

Settings are saved using browser localStorage, including theme, favorites, and last-played tracks.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Google APIs and OAuth for secure Drive file streaming. Revoke access from your Google account settings anytime.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Please check this page periodically for changes.
